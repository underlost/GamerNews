About Gamer news

  • Hand crafted by: Tyler Rilling.
  • Follow him on twitter: @underlost
  • You can send email to: tyler@underlost.it.

How?

Gamer news was written in Django, a python web framework. It uses a mix of Redis for storing votes, and memcache for caching everything. PostgreSQL is used for storing profiles, stories and everything else. Static content is hosted on a Media Temple (dv) server, while the app itself is served up on Heroku.

Why?

Mostly just to prove I could. I wanted to create my own version of Reddit/Hacker News/digg/whatever, to gain a better understanding how the internals of such sites worked. I wanted to create a site with the features that I wanted.
